# Financial Reporting Skill

## Key Reports
- P&L: Revenue - Expenses = Profit
- Balance: Assets = Liab + Equity
- Cash Flow: Operating, investing, financing

## Reporting Cadence
- Monthly: P&L, balance
- Weekly: Cash position
- Quarterly: All + board
