# Interview Management Skill

## Interview Stages
1. Recruiter screen: 30 min
2. Manager screen: 30-45 min
3. Team interviews: 2-4 hours
4. Exec/ulture: As needed

## Interview Structure
- 5 min: Intro
- 30 min: Skills/role fit
- 5 min: Candidate Q&A
