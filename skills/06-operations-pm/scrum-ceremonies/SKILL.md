# Scrum Ceremonies Skill

## Ceremonies
- Daily standup: 15 min daily
- Sprint planning: 1-2 hours bi-weekly
- Sprint review: 1 hour bi-weekly
- Retro: 1 hour bi-weekly

## Each Ceremony
- Standup: Blockers, progress
- Planning: Commitments
- Review: Demo, feedback
- Retro: What worked, what didn't
