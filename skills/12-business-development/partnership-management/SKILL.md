# Partnership Management Skill

Comprehensive partnership management including recruitment, enablement, and partner success.

## When to Use This Skill

USE THIS SKILL when:
- Managing partner relationships
- Developing channel partners
- Partner go-to-market
- Partner program design
- "We need partners"
- Partner success management

DO NOT USE for:
- Sales (use sales-suite)
- Marketing campaigns (use marketing-suite)

## Partnership Types

| Type | Description | Revenue Model |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Referral | Send leads | Commission |
| Reseller | Sell product | Margin |
| Integrator | Build integrations | Revenue share |
| Strategic | Co-sell | Custom |

```markdown

### Warning Signs
- [ ] Revenue decline >20%
- [ ] Inactive >60 days
- [ ] Support tickets increasing
- [ ] Competitor mentions

### Intervention Triggers
- [ ] Monthly review if revenue down
- [ ] Call if inactive >45 days
- [ ] Escalation if churn risk

1. [ ] Understand root cause
2. [ ] Executive sponsor outreach
3. [ ] Special incentives
4. [ ] Product feedback incorporation
```
