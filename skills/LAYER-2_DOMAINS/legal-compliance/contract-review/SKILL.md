# Contract Review Skill

Legal contract assessment.

## Contract Types

| Contract | Use | Key Clauses |
|----------|-----|------------|
| NDA | Confidentiality | Term, scope, remedies |
| MSA | Master agreement | Terms, payment, liability |
| SOW | Scope of work | Deliverables, timeline |
| SLA | Service levels | Uptime, support |
| License | IP rights | Usage, derivatives |

## Red Flags

| Issue | Risk | Clause |
|-------|------|--------|
| Unlimited liability | High | Cap damages |
| Perpetual IP | Medium | Terminated license |
| Auto-renew | Low | Notice period |
| Broad indemnity | High | Limit scope |

## Risk Levels

| Factor | Low Risk | High Risk |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Liability | Capped | Unlimited |
| Termination | Any time | For cause only |
| IP | Us | Them |
| Governing Law | Home state | Remote |

## Boilerplate Check

- [ ] Parties identified
- [ ] Term defined
- [ ] Termination clear
- [ ] Liability capped
- [ ] Governing law
- [ ] Dispute resolution
- [ ] Force majeure
