# Risk Management Skill

## Risk Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Financial | Cash flow, revenue |
| Operational | Systems, supply chain |
| Compliance | Legal, regulatory |
| Reputational | Brand, PR |

## Risk Matrix

| Probability | Impact | Rating |
|------------|--------|--------|
| High | High | Critical |
| High | Low | Monitor |
| Low | High | Contingency |
| Low | Low | Accept |

## Mitigation Strategies

- Avoid: Change approach
- Transfer: Insurance, partners
- Mitigate: Reduce probability
- Accept: Budget for impact
