# Strategic Planning Skill

Comprehensive strategic planning including vision setting, OKR management, and annual planning cycles.

## When to Use This Skill

USE THIS SKILL when:
- Setting company vision or mission
- Creating annual or quarterly plans
- Defining OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)
- Strategic planning sessions
- "What's our strategy?"
- "How do we plan for next year?"
- Building roadmap
- Competitive positioning

DO NOT USE for:
- Day-to-day operations (use operations-suite)
- Financial planning (use finance-suite)
- Product roadmap (use product-suite)

## Strategy Framework

Follow this linear framework:

```
Vision (Where we want to be)
    ↓
Mission (Why we exist)
    ↓
Goals (What we want to achieve)
    ↓
Strategies (How we'll get there)
    ↓
Tactics (Specific actions)
```

### Step 1: Define Vision

**Vision Statement Template:**

The vision should answer:
- Where will we be in 3-5 years?
- What market will we dominate?
- How will the world be different because of us?

```markdown
## Vision Statement

In [YEAR], [COMPANY] will be the leading [CATEGORY] solution for [TARGET_MARKET], enabling [OUTCOME] through [DIFFERENTIATOR].

[Example]
In 2028, Acme will be the leading AI-powered customer support platform for mid-market SaaS companies, enabling 10x faster resolution times through intelligent automation.
```

### Step 2: Define Mission

**Mission should answer:**
- Why do we exist?
- Who do we serve?
- How do we create value?

```markdown
## Mission Statement

[COMPANY] exists to [PROBLEM_we_solve] for [TARGET_CUSTOMER], so they can [OUTCOME].

[Example]
Acme exists to eliminate customer support frustration for SaaS founders, so they can focus on building great products.
```

### Step 3: Set Goals

Goals should be:
- **Specific** - Clear and defined
- **Measurable** - Quantifiable
- **Aligned** - Connected to vision
- **Time-bound** - Deadline

**Goal Categories:**

| Category | Examples | Timeline |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Revenue | $10M ARR | 2 years |
| Market | #1 in segment | 3 years |
| Product | Launch X features | 1 year |
| Team | Build X functions | 1 year |

### Step 4: Develop Strategies

Strategies answer "HOW":

```markdown
## Strategic Pillars

1. [Pillar Name]
   - Hypothesis: [Why this will work]
   - Investments: [Resources needed]
   - Metrics: [How we'll measure]

2. [Pillar Name]
   - Hypothesis: [Why this will work]
   - Investments: [Resources needed]
   - Metrics: [How we'll measure]
```

### Step 5: Define Tactics

Tactics are specific, actionable items:

```markdown
## Q1 Tactics

| Initiative | Owner | Deadline | metric |
|-----------|-------|----------|--------|
| Launch X feature | @name | Jan 15 | 100 users |
| Hire 2 AEs | @name | Jan 31 | Hired |
```

## OKR Framework

OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) are how we track progress.

### OKR Structure

```markdown
---
O: [Objective - What we want to achieve]
---

KR1: [Key Result] - [metric] from [current] to [target] by [date]
KR2: [Key Result] - [metric] from [current] to [target] by [date]  
KR3: [Key Result] - [metric] from [current] to [target] by [date]
```

### OKR Examples by Department

**Sales OKRs:**
```markdown
O: Achieve $5M ARR with enterprise customers

KR1: Close 10 enterprise deals (>$100K each) from current 3 by Q4
KR2: Increase avg deal size from $50K to $75K by Q3
KR3: Reduce sales cycle from 90 to 60 days by Q2
```

**Marketing OKRs:**
```markdown
O: Generate 500 qualified leads per month

KR1: Increase organic traffic from 10K to 50K visitors by Q4
KR2: Improve conversion rate from 2% to 5% by Q3
KR3: Generate 100 MQLs per month from current 50 by Q2
```

**Product OKRs:**
```markdown
O: Launch market-winning 2.0 product

KR1: Release core 2.0 features by Q2
KR2: Achieve NPS 50+ from current 35 by Q3
KR3: Reduce churn to <5% annually from 12% by Q4
```

### OKR Best Practices

DO:
- Limit to 3-5 objectives per quarter
- Each objective 3-5 key results
- Key results must be measurable
- 70% achievement = success (not 100%)
- Weekly check-ins on progress

DON'T:
- Copy goals to key results
- Set key results as tasks
- Have >5 key results per objective
- Set unmeasurable outcomes
- Penalize for missing targets

## Annual Planning Process

### Timeline

| Quarter | Activity | Output |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Q4 (Month 9-12) | Annual Review & Planning | Next year plan |
| Q1 (Month 1-3) | Kickoff & Alignment | Q1 OKRs |
| Q2 (Month 4-6) | Mid-year Check | Adjustments |
| Q3 (Month 7-9) | Adjustments | Q3 OKRs |
| Q4 (Month 10-12) | Review & Celebrate | Lessons learned |

### Annual Planning Workshop Agenda

```
## Strategy Day Agenda

### Morning: Analysis (3 hours)
- Review: What worked/didn't last year
- Market: Competitive landscape
- Customers: Feedback themes
- Team: Capabilities & gaps

### Afternoon: Planning (3 hours)
- Vision alignment
- Goal setting
- Strategic priorities
- Resource allocation
```
