# Org Design Skill

## Org Structure Options
- Functional: By department
-矩阵: Functional + product
- Divisional: By business unit
- Flat: Minimal hierarchy

## Design Principles
1. Clear reporting
2. Accountabilities defined
3. Right span of control
4. Growth paths clear

## Team Sizing
- Startup: 3-10
- Early: 10-30
- Growth: 30-100
- Scale: 100+

## Mapping Tool
Function → Head → Team Size → Reports To
