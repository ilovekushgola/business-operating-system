# Fundraising Skill

## Fundraising Stages
- Pre-seed: Idea, founding team
- Seed: MVP, early traction
- Series A: Product-market fit
- Series B: Scale
- Series C+: Growth

## Pitch Deck Sections
1. Problem
2. Solution
3. Market opportunity
4. Product
5. Business model
6. Traction
7. Team
8. Competition
9. Financials
10. Ask

## Investor Targets
- Angels: $10K-100K
- Seed: $500K-2M
- Series A: $2M-15M
- Series B: $15M+

## Key Metrics
- ARR, MRR
- Growth rate
- CAC, LTV
- Burn rate, runway
