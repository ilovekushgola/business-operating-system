# Board Management Skill

Comprehensive board management including governance, meeting management, and board relations.

## When to Use This Skill

USE THIS SKILL when:
- Preparing for board meetings
- Board governance questions
- Board relations and communication
- Director recruitment
- Board materials preparation
- Governance compliance
- "How do we prepare for board?"
- "What do we report to the board?"
- Board meeting scheduling

DO NOT USE for:
- Investor relations (use investor-relations)
- Executive communication (use c-level-advisor)
- Financial reporting (use finance-suite)

## Board Meeting Types

### Regular Meetings (Monthly/Quarterly)

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Focus |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Board | Monthly or Quarterly | 2-4 hours | Strategic |
| Committee | As needed | 1-2 hours | Specific area |
| Executive Session | As needed | 30-60 min | Closed door |

### Special Meetings
- **Strategy Offsite**: Annual, full day
- **Emergency**: As needed, short notice
- **Election**: Board member changes

## Board Meeting Agenda Template

```
## Board Meeting Agenda
Date: [DATE]
Time: [TIME]
Location: [IN-PERSON/ZOOM]

### 1. Call to Order (5 min)
- Chair calls meeting to order
- Note quorum present

### 2. Approval of Minutes (5 min)
- Review [DATE] minutes
- Motion: Approve minutes
- Vote

### 3. CEO Report (15 min)
- Highlights
- Key metrics
- Strategic updates
- Questions

### 4. Financial Report (15 min)
- P&L summary
- Cash position
- Runway
- Questions

### 5. Committee Reports (30 min)
- [Committee 1]: [Updates]
- [Committee 2]: [Updates]

### 6. Old Business (10 min)
- [Pending items]

### 7. New Business (30 min)
- [Item 1]: Discussion + Vote
- [Item 2]: Discussion + Vote

### 8. Adjournment
- Motion to adjourn
- Next meeting date
```

## Board Materials

### Board Deck Structure

Each board meeting needs comprehensive materials delivered 7 days before:

```markdown

1. Executive Summary
2. Financials
3. Operations
4. Product
5. People
6. Strategic Items

- One page summary of company state

- Key challenges

## Board Governance

### Fiduciary Duties

Directors have three fiduciary duties:

| Duty | Description | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| Duty of Care | Actively participate | Attend meetings, ask questions |
| Duty of Loyalty | Put company first | No conflicts, disclose |
| Duty of Obedience | Follow laws/regs | Comply with SOX, etc |

### Board Committees

Typical committees and responsibilities:

| Committee | Purpose | Members |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Audit | Financial oversight | 3+ independent |
| Compensation | Exec pay, equity | As appropriate |
| Governance | Board practices | As appropriate |
| Nominating | Director selection | As appropriate |

### Committee Charters (Summary)

**Audit Committee:**
- Review quarterly financials
- Oversee external auditor
- Monitor internal controls
- Risk management

**Compensation Committee:**
- CEO compensation
- Executive compensation
- Equity plans
- Employee bonus programs

## Director Management

### Director Recruitment Process

```
1. Define Needs
   - Skills gaps
   - Diversity goals
   - Time commitment

2. Pipeline Development
   - Network referrals
   - Executive search
   - Personal outreach

3. Screening
   - Initial call
   - In-person meetings
   - Reference checks

4. Nomination
   - Board discussion
   - Vote

5. Onboarding
   - Background check
