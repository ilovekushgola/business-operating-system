# Customer Communication Skill

## Communication Best Practices
- Acknowledge: Show you understand
- Apologize: Be sorry it happened
- Solve: Fix the problem
- Follow up: Make sure it's resolved

## Templates
- Acknowledge: "I understand how frustrating..."
- Resolution: "Here's what we did..."
- Follow up: "Just checking in..."
