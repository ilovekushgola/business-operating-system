# Churn Prevention Skill

## Churn Signals
- Usage drop: <50% decline
- Support increase: More tickets
- NPS drop: Detractor
- Non-renewal: Won't renew

## Retention Playbook
- At-risk: Proactive outreach
- Win-back: Special offer + check-in
- Departure survey: Learn why
