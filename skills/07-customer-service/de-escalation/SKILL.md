# De-escalation Skill

## De-escalation Steps
1. Listen: Let them vent
2. Empathize: Acknowledge feelings
3. Apologize: Sincerely
4. Solve: Fix or offer options
5. Retain: Save the relationship

## Key Phrases
- "I understand why you're upset"
- "I would feel the same way"
- "Here's what I can do..."
