# SLA Management Skill

## SLA Tiers
- P1 (Critical): 15 min response
- P2 (High): 1 hour response
- P3 (Normal): 4 hour response
- P4 (Low): 24 hour response

## SLA Metrics
- Response time: First reply
- Resolution time: Full fix
- CSAT: Satisfaction score
