# Ticket Management Skill

## Ticket Lifecycle
1. New: Created
2. Open: Assigned
3. Pending: Waiting on customer
4. Solved: Fixed
5. Closed: Resolved

## Ticket Priority
- Urgent: Outage
- High: Major issue
- Normal: Standard
- Low: Feature request
