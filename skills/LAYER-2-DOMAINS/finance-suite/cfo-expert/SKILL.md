## Fundraising Framework

### Preparation
```
Metrics to Prepare:
- MRR/ARR Growth
- Gross Margin
- CAC Payback
- LTV/CAC
- Burn Rate
- Runway
```

### Valuation Model
```
- ARR Multiple (for SaaS)
- Revenue Multiple (for other)
- Discounted Cash Flow
- Comparables
```

### Investor Deck Metrics
| Metric | Definition | Good | Great |
|-------|-----------|------|-------|
| ARR Growth | YoY Growth | 100%+ | 200%+ |
| Gross Margin | Revenue - COGS | 70%+ | 80%+ |
| CAC Payback | Months to recover | <12 | <6 |
| LTV/CAC | Return ratio | >3 | >5 |
