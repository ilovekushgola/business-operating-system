# Growth Marketing Skill

## Growth Framework
1. Ideas: Team, competitors
2. Prioritize: Impact, effort
3. Test: Run experiment
4. Learn: Analyze results
5. Scale: Double down

## Common Experiments
- Subject lines
- CTA buttons
- Landing pages
- Pricing pages
- Referral programs

## Viral Loops
- Product: Share after value
- Referral: Incentivize
- Content: Templates, tools
