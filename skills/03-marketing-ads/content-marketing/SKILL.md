# Content Marketing Skill

## Content Types
- Blog: SEO, thought leadership
- Case studies: Social proof
- Whitepapers: Lead magnet
- Webinars: Product education
- Video: Engagement
- Infographics: Visual data

## Content Funnel
- TOFU: Awareness (blogs, social)
- MOFU: Consideration (case studies)
- BOFU: Decision (demos, trials)
