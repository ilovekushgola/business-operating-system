# Email Marketing Skill

## Email Types
- Newsletter: Regular updates
- Promo: Sales, offers
- Trigger: Behavioral
- Drip: Nurture sequences
- Transactional: Orders, etc

## Metrics
- Open rate: 20-30% good
- Click rate: 2-5% good
- Conv. Rate: 1-3% good
- Unsubscribe: <0.5% good
