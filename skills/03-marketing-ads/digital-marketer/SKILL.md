# Digital Marketer Skill

Comprehensive digital marketing covering SEO, content, paid ads, social, and analytics.

## When to Use This Skill

USE THIS SKILL when:
- Creating digital marketing strategy
- Planning campaigns across channels
- SEO and content strategy
- Paid advertising (Google, Meta, LinkedIn)
- Social media marketing
- Marketing analytics
- "How should we market online?"
- "What's our digital strategy?"
- Campaign performance

DO NOT USE for:
- Offline marketing (use traditional)
- PR/Communications (use marketing-suite)
- Events (use event-management)
- Product marketing (use product-suite)

## SEO Strategy

### SEO Framework

```
1. Technical Audit → 2. Keyword Research → 3. Content Strategy → 4. Link Building → 5. Measure
```

### Keyword Research

**Keyword Types:**

| Type | Volume | Intent | Difficulty |
|------|--------|-------|------------|
| Head terms | High | Informational | Hard |
| Body terms | Medium | Commercial | Medium |
| Long-tail | Low | Transactional | Easy |

**Keyword Research Process:**

```markdown
### Step 1: Seed Keywords
List core topics: [Product category, use cases, industry terms]

### Step 2: Expansion
Use tools: Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, SEMrush

### Step 3: Classification
| Keyword | Intent | Volume | Difficulty | Opportunity |
|----------|--------|-------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| [term] | [Trans/Nav/Comm] | [X/month] | [X/100] | [H/M/L] |

### Step 4: Prioritization
Prioritize: High intent + Low difficulty + High opportunity
```

### On-Page SEO Checklist

```markdown
### Title Tags
- Include primary keyword
- 50-60 characters
- Unique per page

### Meta Descriptions
- Include keyword
- Call to action
- 150-160 characters

### Headings
- H1: One per page (primary keyword)
- H2-H6: Supporting keywords

### Content
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- 1-2% keyword density
- Related keywords throughout
- Internal links

### Images
- Alt text with keywords
- Compressed
- Descriptive filenames
```

### Technical SEO Audit

| Issue | Impact | Fix |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Slow page speed | High | Optimize images, CDN |
| Duplicate content | Medium | Canonical tags |
| Missing meta | Medium | Add title/description |
| Broken links | Low | Fix 404s |
| No XML sitemap | Medium | Generate sitemap |
| Mobile unfriendly | High | Responsive design |

## Paid Advertising

### Google Ads Structure

```
Account
└── Campaigns (by objective)
    └── Ad Groups (by theme)
        └── Keywords + Ads
```

### Campaign Types

| Objective | Best Campaign | Goal |
|-----------|--------------|------|
| Sales | Search | Conversions |
| Leads | Search/Display | Leads |
| Traffic | Search | Clicks |
| Awareness | Display/YouTube | Impressions |
| Ecommerce | Shopping | Sales |

### Keyword Match Types

| Type | Syntax | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| Broad | [keyword] | women's hats |
| Phrase | "women's hats" | "women's hats" |
| Exact | [women's hats] | [women's hats] |

**Recommendation:** Use exact for high performers, phrase for testing.

### Ad Copy Templates

**Search Ad:**
```
Headlines (30 char each):
- Headline 1: [Benefit/USP]
- Headline 2: [Social proof]
- Headline 3: [CTA]

Descriptions (90 char each):
- Description 1: [Problem + Solution]
- Description 2: [Features + CTA]
```

**Responsive Search Ad Best Practices:**
- Include keyword in headlines
- Add all 15 headlines
- Use variety: benefit, feature, CTA
- Test clearly different ads

**Conversion:**
- Leads generated
- MQLs created
- Deals influenced
- Revenue attributed

### Marketing Attribution

| Model | Description | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------------|------|------|
| First touch | First channel gets credit | Simple | Overcredits awareness |
| Last touch | Last channel gets credit | Simple | Ignores research |
| Linear | Equal credit | Fair | Doesn't weight value |
| Position | First + last get credit | Balanced | Arbitrary weights |
| Time decay | Recent gets more | Accounts for length | Complex |
| Data-driven | ML determines | Accurate | Needs data |
