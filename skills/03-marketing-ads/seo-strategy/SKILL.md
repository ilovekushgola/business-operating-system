# SEO Strategy Skill

## SEO Pillars
1. Technical: Site speed, index
2. On-page: Content, keywords
3. Off-page: Links, mentions

## Keyword Research
- Head terms: High vol, high comp
- Long-tail: Low vol, low comp
- LSI: Semantic variations

## Content Optimization
- Title: Primary keyword
- H1: Primary keyword
- H2: Secondary keywords
- Body: Natural keyword density
- Meta: Description + keyword
