# Company Context

Business knowledge, vision, values, and organizational memory.

## Purpose

Maintain comprehensive company knowledge including:
- Vision and mission
- Values and culture
- Organizational structure
- Key contacts
- Products and services
- History and milestones

## Usage

**Load company context:**
- Ask about company vision
- Reference company values
- Look up team structure
- Find product information

**Include in context:**
- Company overview for strategic decisions
- Organizational charts for hiring decisions
- Product details for sales/support

## Data Structure

```markdown
## Company Overview
- Founded: [Date]
- Industry: [Industry]
- Stage: [Seed/Series A/etc]
- Size: [Employees]

## Vision
[Company vision statement]

## Values
- [Value 1]
- [Value 2]
- [Value 3]

## Products
| Product | Description | Stage |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| [Name] | [Desc] | [Live/Beta] |

## Leadership
| Role | Name | Since |
|------|-----|-------|
| CEO | [Name] | [Date] |
| CTO | [Name] | [Date] |

## Key Metrics
- ARR: $[Amount]
- Customers: [Count]
- Team: [Count]
```
