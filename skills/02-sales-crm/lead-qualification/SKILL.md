# Lead Qualification Skill

## BANT Framework
- Budget: Do they have budget?
- Authority: Can they decide?
- Need: Do they need it?
- Timeline: When do they buy?

## MEDDIC Framework
- Metrics: What are their KPIs?
- Economic Buyer: Who pays?
- Decision Criteria: How do they choose?
- Decision Process: How do they buy?
- Implication: What's the cost of delay?
- Champion: Who advocates?

## Qualification Questions
1. What problem are you solving?
2. What's your timeline?
3. who's involved in the decision?
4. What's budget?
5. What happens if you don't solve it?

- Company size
- Industry
- Tech stack
- Use case fit
