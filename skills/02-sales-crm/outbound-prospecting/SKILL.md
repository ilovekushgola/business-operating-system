# Outbound Prospecting Skill

## Prospecting Channels
- Cold email: Highest volume
- Cold calling: Highest conversion
- LinkedIn: Research + touch
- Events: High intent

## Email Sequence Template
Day 0: Initial email
Day 2: Value add
Day 4: Social touch
Day 7: Break-up

## Call Script
```
Hi [Name], this is [Name] from [Company].
[Quick intro]
[Question about their business]
[Brief value prop]
[Ask: Any sense of timing?]
```

## Best Times to Prospect
- Tue-Thu: 9-11am, 4-5pm
- Mon: After morning team meeting
- Avoid: Mon am, Fri, lunch
