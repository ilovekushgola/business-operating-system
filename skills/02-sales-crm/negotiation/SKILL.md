# Negotiation Skill

## Negotiation Principles
1. Never give without getting
2. Know your walk-away
3. Multiple issues, not single
4. Written terms > verbal
5. Close is the goal

## Trading Points
- Price ↓ = Terms ↑ (length, seats)
- Price ↓ = Features ↓
- Price ↓ = Timeline ↓

## Common Concessions
- Demo/trial length
- Implementation services
- Seat count
- Price/term
- Support level

## Closing Techniques
- Assumptive: "Let's get this signed"
- Urgency: "This expires Friday"
- Scarcity: "Last spot this quarter"
- Summary: Recap value
