# Facility Management Skill

Comprehensive facility and office management including operations, vendors, and administration.

## When to Use This Skill

USE THIS SKILL when:
- Managing office facilities
- Vendor coordination
- Office vendor relations
- Office operations
- Facilities purchasing
- Security/access management

DO NOT USE for:
- HR (use hr-suite)
- IT support (use tech-suite)

## Office Operations

### Daily Operations Checklist

```markdown
## Daily Facility Checklist

### Morning (8-9 AM)
- [ ] Check lobby/package area
- [ ] Verify restrooms stocked
- [ ] Check conference rooms
- [ ] Verify kitchen supplies

### Throughout Day
- [ ] Handle deliveries
- [ ] Respond to facility requests
- [ ] Coordinate with vendors

### End of Day
- [ ] Secure building
- [ ] Turn off lights/hvac
```
